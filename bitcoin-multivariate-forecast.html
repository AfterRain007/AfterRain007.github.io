Time Series Forecasting Transformers Financial Analysis Deep Learning Python

How does one forecast volatile Bitcoin prices?

Predicting Bitcoin prices accurately is a complex task due to its high volatility. This project explores the efficacy of Temporal Fusion Transformers (TFTs) in forecasting Bitcoin prices. We compare TFTs with LSTM, GRU, and TCN models, while incorporating Volume, Sentiment, and Google Trends as key features to enhance predictive performance.

Data Preprocessing

Outlier Handling (Simple Moving Average, Boxplot)

The importance of data preprocessing for optimal model performance can't go unnoticed. Outlier handling using Simple Moving Average and Boxplot, also data normalization using First Order Differencing Transformation is explored to manage volatility.

Based on the result of our test, when using Boxplot TFT pulls forward compared to other traditional model. But thing changes when we use moving average as our outlier handling method. TFT is ahead in terms of Root Mean Squared Error, but TCN is better in terms of Mean Absolute Error and Mean Absolute Percentage Error. Average training time of the model is also compared and TCN is better by more than 3 times compared to TFT.

White Box

Temporal Fusion Transformer's Autoencoder Explainability

One of many advantages of TFT are also it's explainability. Rather than other traditional architecture, we are able to see which one of the variable that's more important compared to other in the encoder and also decoder. As we can see the Trend take the number one spot followed by linear increase and week in the encoder. On the decoder part, trend also take the number one spot, and followed by week also volume.

Temporal Fusion Transformer's Attention Mean Explainability

We can also see the attention mechanism, it peaks at -7 relative to the first prediction point. Which mean it's using the last seven data points for accurate forecasts or in other words data from the last 7 days (1 week) is crucial for TFT to generate accurate forecasts.

Conclusion

The analysis demonstrates that TFT using Boxplot as its outlier handling achieve superior Bitcoin price prediction, albeit with longer training times. They provide valuable insights into variable importance. This research highlights TFTs' potential for high accuracy and interpretability in time series forecasting.
