Data Analytics Much?

In February of 2024, I had the opportunity to participate in a scholarship program offered by Talenta AI Indonesia. This program focused on Microsoft Azure Data Fundamentals and Advanced Power BI. At the conclusion of the program, I was selected among 200 participants across Indonesia to receive a complimentary Microsoft Certification. I successfully passed the Microsoft Certification: Power BI Data Analyst Associate with a score of 793 out of 1000.

Leveraging the knowledge and skills gained through this program, I've since created numerous dashboards during my internship at Asia Pulp & Paper as a Power BI Developer and at Unilever as a Customer Development!
