In February of 2024, I had the opportunity to participate in a scholarship program offered by Talenta AI Indonesia. This program focused on Microsoft Azure Data Fundamentals and Advanced Power BI. Leveraging the knowledge and skills gained through this program, I've since created numerous dashboards during my internship at Asia Pulp & Paper as a Power BI Developer and at Unilever as a Customer Development!

Asia Pulp & Paper

Security Incident Dashboard

This Power BI dashboard was built to support the Corporate Resilience Division in monitoring and analyzing security incidents across multiple sites. It provides visual breakdowns of incident types, frequency by location, and item-specific case distribution using interactive charts and filters. With the ability to drill down by mill name and review trends over time, the dashboard equips stakeholders with timely insights to drive preventive actions, improve incident reporting quality, and enhance operational security across facilities.

Unilever Indonesia

Enrollment Dashboard

This Power BI dashboard was designed to provide a clear and interactive overview of enrollment progress across multiple regions. It features visual comparisons between actual and target enrollments, allowing users to quickly assess performance at both a monthly and year-to-date level. A geospatial map is included to track enrollment distribution by location and concession, helping teams identify geographic trends and gaps in coverage. Key summary indicators and trend charts provide immediate visibility into campaign progress and time remaining to meet goals.

To support data analysis and operational follow-up, a second page offers a comprehensive tabular view with export functionality. This enables stakeholders to easily filter, sort, and extract the underlying data for further reporting or field actions. The dashboard is designed with usability in mind, ensuring fast access to insights and facilitating timely decision-making for both strategic planning and day-to-day execution. Its structure encourages accountability while improving transparency across teams involved in the enrollment initiative.
