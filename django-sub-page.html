General Overview

Django App to Track Sales Performance.

Comprehensive Breakdown

Django App that handles Create, Read, Update and Delete (CRUD) of a Sales Person with Python and MySQL. It creates simplicity by just inputting the name of employee, employment status, and performance. It automatically assign different target according to it’s employment status and concludes the status based on the target and performance.
