Pot Holes Detection with Computer Vision
Pot Holes, Pot Holes everywhere!
Indonesia Pot Holes (IPOTH)

General Overview

Based on the problem Indonesia have which is pothole, me and four of my friends created a computer vision model using YOLO to automatically detect pothole. After training, we got mAP results on IoU 50 training of 84% and 52% on testing with only 30 epochs and publicly available dataset. Not only that, but we actually made a mock up application too!

Comprehensive Breakdown

Me and four of my friends which are Muhammad Hanan Azhar Jihaannuriy, Izzuddin, Rizky Azriel Fahrezi, and Najib Ridho Sandika joined an international competition called World Youth Invention and Innovation Award in the Technology Category. Based on the traffic accident report in Indonesia which cost 698 lives where infrastructure is the third most accident cause, we decided to do computer vision as our project. With that in mind, we use Annotated Potholes Dataset by Atikur Rahman Chitholian dataset to built a YOLO model with it. 532 annotated image were used for training and 133 for testing, but due to hardware and time limitations we can only do 30 epochs for the training phase. Nonetheless it results in mAP results on IoU 50 training of 84% and 52% on testing! After we built our model, a mock up mobile application was made to further solidify our idea. And at the end we got a gold medal at World Youth Invention and Innovation Award in the Technology Category!
